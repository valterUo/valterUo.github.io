Bio

I am soon graduating master's student in mathematics at the University of Helsinki in Finland. I have been focusing on algebraic topics in mathematics such as semi-algebraic geometry and category theory. I have been working as a research assistant in the unified database management systems research group in the computer science department where we have developed some initial ideas on how to model multi-model databases with applied category theory.

Please note that this webpage is not a CV but rather a glance at my academic career, research, and my research interests. It also serves as an archive for the presentations that are otherwise difficult to share but asked often afterward.

Currently working on

I am increasingly interested in quantum computing. Currently, the database community does not realize the possible applications of quantum computing in our field. I am interested in researching these possibilities. Since I have a category-theoretic background, I am also enthusiastic about applying and modeling quantum computational topics using category theory. There is a lot to learn!
